ABOUT

Rate My Course is built for college students. Relating to others interactions on the site has become a key way for students to deal with choosing courses to take. The site does what students have been doing forever - checking in with each other – their friends, their brothers, their sisters, their classmates – to figure out which classes are best and which ones you might want to avoid.
